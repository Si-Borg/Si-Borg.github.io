Early Life

It all started on a rainy night in Chicago, 1983...

Not really, I'm from Georgia, and I grew up half an hour from the closest grocery store, and much further from a good internet connection! Most of my time was spent with the various birds we raised and playing in the woods. I had a GameCube, a Gameboy, a DS, and my brother's old XBox. That was about all the interest I had in technology with the dialup internet connection we had.

Recent Years

As I got older, I took more of an interest in playing games on computers, like Heroes of Might and Magic 2, Insaniquarium, and Minecraft. Before I knew it, I was building a PC of my own and wanting to learn all about technology! I decided to pursue a career in software development, and the ease with which you can create good-looking GUIs with HTML and CSS made web development sound like a great option. Once I decided my direction, I started attending Georgia Northwester Technical College and working towards an Associate's in website design and development, which I have since finished!

Current Time

As I mentioned before, I enjoy technology, so I am always seeking to deepen my understanding of the concepts that undergird programming and not just the syntax of a single language. I love to learn in a hands-on way, with real world problems. I talk myself through a lot of the problems I encounter and research when I feel like I don't know enough to tackle them yet. I enjoy learning from those who have more experience and I can come off as quiet because I like to listen before I speak.

My Future

I would like to gain more experience with back-end development, but my end goal is to be a well-rounded full-stack developer. I want to learn more so I can contribute to some of the open source projects that have helped me. I know I still have so much more to learn, so I look forward to learning about this ever growing and changing field for the rest of my life!
